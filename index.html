Streetz Café is a little, low-key coffee shop with a cozy vibe that is tucked away in the busy old railway near Central Philippine University. Nestled away from the main stream, this undiscovered treasure greets guests with a cozy atmosphere that features exposed soft lighting, and a subtle murmur of conversation. Streetz Café menu has a carefully chosen assortment of small-batch, artisanal coffees as well as a wide range of mouthwatering pastries to suit a wide range of palates. The low-key ambience encourages customers to relax, catch up on studies or work, or have private discussions. Streetz Cafe is a retreat for anyone looking for a break from the fast-paced school life, providing a real and unpretentious coffee experience with its subtle charm and commitment to quality.

Caramel Macciato₱40
The Caramel Macchiato is a luscious espresso-based treat that combines 1-2 shots of espresso with about 6 ounces of steamed milk for a velvety texture. A sweet twist comes from 1-2 tablespoons of vanilla syrup, and the finishing touch is an artful drizzle of caramel. If you prefer it chilled, opt for an Iced Caramel Macchiato. Indulge in the perfect harmony of bold coffee and sweet caramel bliss in every sip.
Spanish Latte₱35
The Spanish Latte is a delightful coffee fusion featuring one or two shots of strong espresso blended with condensed milk. This creates a rich and sweet flavor profile, offering a satisfying balance between the boldness of espresso and the indulgent creaminess of condensed milk. Whether enjoyed hot or iced, it's a deliciously unique coffee experience
Dark Choco₱40
The Dark Chocolate is a decadent coffee treat with a rich and indulgent flavor. It typically starts with one or two shots of espresso as its robust base. Dark chocolate syrup is then added, imparting a deep and intense cocoa flavor. Steamed milk provides a creamy and velvety texture, completing the luxurious experience. The Dark Chocolate is a perfect choice for those who crave a harmonious blend of bold coffee and the irresistible allure of dark chocolate.
Vanilla Latte₱40
The Vanilla Latte is a delightful coffee indulgence made with one or two shots of espresso, blended with velvety steamed milk, and sweetened with a touch of vanilla syrup. This harmonious combination creates a smooth and satisfying beverage, perfect for those who appreciate a hint of sweetness in their coffee.
Brownies₱10
Brownies, beloved for their rich and fudgy texture, are crafted with a simple yet indulgent combination of ingredients. This classic treat typically includes unsweetened chocolate, butter, sugar, eggs, and flour. The unsweetened chocolate and butter meld together to create a decadent base, while sugar sweetens the mixture, and eggs contribute to the dense and moist texture. All these components are carefully blended with flour, resulting in the beloved brownie consistency. Often, vanilla extract and a pinch of salt are added to enhance the flavor profile. Whether enjoyed plain or adorned with nuts or chocolate chips, brownies are a timeless dessert delight.
Strawberry Latte₱40
The Strawberry Latte is a delightful coffee creation featuring one or two shots of espresso harmoniously blended with steamed milk and a burst of strawberry flavor. The result is a lusciously sweet and refreshing beverage, perfect for those seeking a fruity twist in their coffee experience.
Hazelnut₱40
The Hazelnut Coffee is a delightful brew that combines the boldness of one or two shots of espresso with the warm, nutty essence of hazelnut flavoring. Steamed milk is added to create a creamy and satisfying texture, resulting in a rich and aromatic coffee experience. This Hazelnut Coffee is the perfect choice for those who appreciate the comforting and nutty undertones in their daily cup of joe
Sandwich₱45
A sandwich, a culinary classic, is a versatile creation with a straightforward yet satisfying combination of ingredients. Typically nestled between two slices of bread, the filling possibilities are endless. A basic sandwich might consist of protein options like turkey, ham, or chicken, complemented by fresh vegetables such as lettuce, tomatoes, and onions. A spread, often mayo or mustard, adds a zesty touch. For those seeking a vegetarian option, various cheeses, avocado, or hummus can offer a delightful twist. The magic of a sandwich lies in its adaptability, making it a quick and customizable meal for any palate.
Matcha₱40
The Matcha Coffee is a unique fusion of robust coffee and vibrant matcha green tea. This distinctive beverage starts with one or two shots of espresso, creating a bold foundation. Matcha powder is then incorporated, infusing the drink with its earthy and slightly bitter notes, offering a delightful contrast to the coffee's richness. The combination is harmonized with steamed milk, resulting in a creamy and invigorating coffee experience with a touch of Japanese tea elegance.
